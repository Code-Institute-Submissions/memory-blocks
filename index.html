Memory Blocks!

A fun and challenging memory game for all ages!

Instructions

1. Pick a level.

- Choose between Easy, Medium & Hard.

2. Click Play.

- You must memorize the grid before the timer runs out.

3. Fill in the grid.

- The hard part! You must fill in the grid by choosing from the colours provided.

4. When the timer runs out the game ends.

- But if you are fast you can click the finish button!

5. Your score will be shown, try to beat it on your next go!

Note: the harder the level the more points you get.

Easy - 10 points per correct square.
Medium - 20 points per correct square.
Hard - 30 points per correct square.
